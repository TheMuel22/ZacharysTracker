Privacy Policy for Zachary's Weaning Tracker

Last updated: December 7, 2025

Overview

Zachary's Weaning Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to help parents track their baby's weaning journey. All data is stored for your personal use only and is not shared, sold, or used for any purpose other than providing the app's functionality.

Information We Collect

The app collects and stores the following information:

  • Food Tracking Data: Information about foods your baby has tried, including food names, dates, ratings, and notes you enter
  • Account Information (Optional): If you choose to enable cloud sync, we collect your email address for authentication purposes only
  • Local Storage Data: The app stores your tracking data locally on your device

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To display your baby's weaning progress within the app
  • To sync your data across your devices (only if you enable cloud sync)
  • To provide app functionality such as summaries, statistics, and tracking history

We do not:

  • Share your data with any third parties
  • Sell your data
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing
  • Access or view your personal tracking data
  • Send you promotional emails or notifications

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: By default, all data is stored locally on your device and never leaves your phone
  • Cloud Storage (Optional): If you choose to enable cloud sync by logging in, your data is stored securely in Google Firebase, encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Security: We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Firebase provides enterprise-level security with encryption

Data Access and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can use the app without creating an account (local storage only)
  • You can export your data at any time using the "Export Data" feature
  • You can delete all your data by uninstalling the app (local data) or by contacting us to delete cloud data
  • Only you can access your tracking data when logged into your account

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Used for optional cloud sync and authentication. Firebase's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

We do not use analytics, advertising networks, or any other third-party tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for parents and caregivers to track their baby's weaning journey. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. All data entered is about your baby's eating habits and is controlled entirely by the parent/caregiver.

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the app or clear the data
  • Cloud Data: Stored in Firebase until you request deletion or delete your account

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time within the app
  • Export your data in JSON format
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Use the app without cloud sync (completely offline)
  • Request deletion of your cloud-stored data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Consent
